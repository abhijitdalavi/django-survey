Welcome to the Fish Market Survey!

You are on a team of surveyors going out into markets each day to talk with fish vendors. You collect fish species and sales information on a mobile phone to send back to biologists and managers to help improve assessment of food security concerns.

Beyond this Demo

  • If data service is unavailable, results can be stored on the device to be submitted later when service is available.
  • Questions and options can be changed easily.

Enjoy the day in the market!
