Welcome to the Commercial Fishing Logbook!

Your Assignment

You lead a crew on a commercial fishing vessel. You are required to submit a logbook of your trip activities to a regulatory group and want that effort to be as accurate, easy and quick as possible. You also want to keep a close eye on state-wide fishing quotas and track your location and catch information for planning purposes.

Beyond this Demo

  • If data service is unavailable, results can be stored on the device to be submitted later when service is available.
  • You can start and end trips as needed to switch logbooks mid-trip.
  • You can record multiple fishing events per trip.
  • You can record the start and end of a line of traps.
